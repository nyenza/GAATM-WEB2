Our Services

GAATM Investment Company Limited offer a wide range of services as described below.

Transport and Logistics

Providing road, air, and sea transportation for goods and passengers, including freight, cargo, and customs services.

Civil Works and Construction

Engaging in building construction, repairs, water systems, and well drilling.

Agro-based and Farming Services

Offering poultry, cattle, fish farming, and agricultural production, including processing, packaging, and marketing.

Feed Mill and Livestock Services

Establishing modern feed mills for poultry, fish, and cattle, and engaging in livestock farming and research.

Real Estate Development

Acquiring, managing, and developing land through farming, mining, and construction projects.

Consultancy and Training

Providing consultancy in agriculture, environment, finance, IT, and management, along with training and coaching services.

Financial and Microfinance Services

Offering banking, loans, and microfinance services to support individual and business financial needs.

Hospitality and Tourism

Operating hotels, lodges, and tourism services, including accommodation, catering, and guided tours.

Import and Export Trade

Importing and exporting goods, including electronics, pharmaceuticals, machinery, and household products..

Environmental Services.

Conducting Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA) and Social Impact Assessments (SIA) for various projects.
